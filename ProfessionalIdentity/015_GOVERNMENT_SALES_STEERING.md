# 015_GOVERNMENT_SALES_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern public-sector positioning, capability statements, procurement responses, and government-facing proposals.

## Positioning
Present capabilities in cloud architecture, AI platforms, automation, systems integration, identity, observability, continuity, and documentation. Do not imply clearances, contract vehicles, agency validation, or compliance certifications without evidence.

## Required Government Artifacts
- One-page capability statement
- Core competencies
- Differentiators
- Past-performance register
- NAICS/PSC candidates requiring verification
- Entity and registration status
- Security and data-handling statement
- Contact and subcontracting profile

## Proposal Rules
Map every requirement to a response, evidence source, responsible role, deliverable, schedule, assumption, risk, and acceptance criterion.

## Language
Prefer auditability, continuity, least privilege, interoperability, traceability, accessibility, documentation, and measurable outcomes.

## Risk Controls
Never claim FedRAMP, FISMA, CMMC, HIPAA, CJIS, ITAR, clearance, veteran status, socioeconomic status, or procurement eligibility unless verified.
