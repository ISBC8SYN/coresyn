# 029_WHITEPAPER_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern authoritative technical white papers used for enterprise education, consulting proof, partnership discussions, and product positioning.

## Required Structure
Title, audience, executive summary, problem, scope, definitions, current-state analysis, proposed architecture or method, evidence, tradeoffs, risks, implementation roadmap, conclusion, and references.

## Evidence
Cite primary sources where available. Distinguish repository evidence, external research, original interpretation, and future hypothesis.

## Quality Gates
Technical review, factual review, security/privacy review, claim review, accessibility, and executive readability.

## Commercial Rule
A white paper should educate first and create a natural path to an assessment, case study, or consulting engagement without becoming a disguised sales brochure.
