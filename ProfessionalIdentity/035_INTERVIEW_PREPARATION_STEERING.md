# 035_INTERVIEW_PREPARATION_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern preparation for recruiter, hiring-manager, architecture, system-design, behavioral, consulting, and executive interviews.

## Evidence Story Format
Situation, constraint, responsibility, decision, implementation, validation, result, tradeoff, and lesson.

## Required Story Bank
AWS architecture, AI integration, CI/CD improvement, automation, security, incident or failure, disagreement, ambiguous requirements, documentation, cost tradeoff, recovery, and stakeholder communication.

## System Design Approach
Clarify goals, users, scale, constraints, data, trust boundaries, availability, failure modes, cost, observability, operations, and evolution before choosing services.

## Interview Rules
Be candid about gaps, distinguish architecture from hands-on work, avoid proprietary details, and never improvise unsupported metrics.

## Closing Questions
Ask about success measures, architecture ownership, operational maturity, decision authority, current risks, team interfaces, and the first ninety days.
