# 016_HEALTHCARE_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern healthcare-facing architecture, automation, and consulting materials.

## Focus Areas
Clinical and administrative workflow integration, secure identity, interoperability, operational automation, scheduling, documentation, reporting, resilience, and AI-assisted support.

## Safety Boundary
Do not provide medical diagnosis, represent software as a medical device, or claim HIPAA compliance without a formal scope and evidence. Distinguish technical safeguards from legal compliance.

## Required Design Questions
What data is handled? Who may access it? What is the clinical decision boundary? What requires human approval? How are access, audit, retention, backup, and incident response managed?

## Public Language
Use privacy-aware architecture, minimum necessary access, auditability, workflow support, interoperability, continuity, and human oversight.

## Case Study Requirements
Sanitize all protected health information. Show process, architecture, controls, operational result, and limitations without exposing patients or providers.
