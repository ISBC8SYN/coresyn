# 022_PUBLIC_SPEAKING_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern speaker biographies, talk proposals, conference abstracts, webinars, workshops, and interview appearances.

## Core Topics
AI platform architecture, AWS systems design, DevOps reliability, automation, operational AI, identity, observability, resilient platforms, and translating complex architecture for executives.

## Talk Structure
Audience problem, key idea, practical framework, evidence or example, implementation guidance, risks, and actionable takeaway.

## Required Assets
50-word bio, 100-word bio, headshot, topic list, three abstracts, learning objectives, audience level, AV needs, contact information, and recording permission.

## Quality Rules
Teach before selling. Avoid unsupported claims, private methodology names, confidential details, and unexplained jargon. Use diagrams and concrete examples.
