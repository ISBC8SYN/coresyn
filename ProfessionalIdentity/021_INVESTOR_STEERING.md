# 021_INVESTOR_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern investor, partner, and commercialization narratives without overstating maturity or valuation.

## Narrative Order
Problem, target customer, current capability, evidence, business model, differentiation, adoption path, competitive alternatives, risks, roadmap, team needs, and use of funds.

## Evidence Rules
Separate repository assets, prototypes, deployments, revenue, customers, partnerships, and future plans. Never imply traction that is not documented.

## Required Artifacts
One-page overview, pitch deck, product architecture, market hypothesis, pricing model, validation plan, financial assumptions, risk register, IP inventory, and diligence index.

## Valuation Rule
Do not assert a valuation as fact. Present scenario-based assumptions and comparable logic only with current research and explicit caveats.

## Public Boundary
Internal PLM/C6ISR language remains private unless the investment thesis specifically concerns publishing or licensing that methodology.
