# 030_RESEARCH_PAPER_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern formal research papers, experimental reports, and technical hypotheses.

## Required Structure
Abstract, research question, background, related work, hypothesis, methodology, data, experiment, results, limitations, reproducibility, ethics, conclusion, and references.

## Integrity Rules
Do not present internal validation as independent peer review. Do not claim scientific proof from demonstrations alone. Preserve raw data, code, configuration, and negative results where safe and lawful.

## Reproducibility
Record versions, environment, inputs, random seeds, evaluation criteria, statistical assumptions, and instructions sufficient for an independent evaluator.

## Publication Boundary
Professional marketing materials may summarize findings only after maturity, evidence strength, and limitations are clearly stated.
