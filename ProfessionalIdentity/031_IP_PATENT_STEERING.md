# 031_IP_PATENT_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern intellectual-property inventory, publication timing, licensing, confidentiality, and patent-related claims.

## IP Categories
Source code, architecture, documentation, original methods, datasets, branding, domain names, trade secrets, inventions, and third-party components.

## Rules
- Never claim patent-pending status without a filed application.
- Record authorship, creation date, repository location, license, third-party dependencies, disclosure history, and ownership.
- Review public disclosure before patent decisions.
- Do not place confidential invention details in public marketing material.

## Required Registers
IP inventory, license inventory, contributor record, disclosure log, assignment record, and candidate invention brief.

## Legal Boundary
This file supports organization and attorney review; it is not a substitute for legal advice or a patentability opinion.
