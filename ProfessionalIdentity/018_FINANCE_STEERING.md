# 018_FINANCE_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern financial-services positioning for cloud, AI, automation, data, identity, audit, and operational resilience.

## Focus Areas
Secure data flows, access controls, transaction-support systems, reporting, model governance, observability, continuity, cost controls, and integration.

## Claims Boundary
Do not claim regulatory compliance, fiduciary authority, investment performance, fraud prevention guarantees, or production financial-processing experience without evidence.

## Architecture Questions
What data and transactions exist? Who authorizes actions? What requires dual control? How are records reconciled, retained, audited, recovered, and monitored?

## AI Rules
AI may assist analysis and workflow but must not be represented as guaranteeing financial outcomes. Document model limitations, human approval, logging, and data lineage.

## Preferred Language
Auditability, traceability, segregation of duties, resilience, data lineage, controlled automation, and operational risk reduction.
