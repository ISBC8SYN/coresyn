# 037_SKILLS_MATRIX_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Maintain a precise matrix of technical, architectural, consulting, leadership, and industry capabilities.

## Rating Model
0 Not claimed; 1 Awareness; 2 Working knowledge; 3 Hands-on implementation; 4 Advanced implementation and design; 5 Principal-level architecture and mentorship.

## Required Fields
Skill, domain, rating, years or recency, evidence paths, projects, interview example, target-role relevance, learning gap, and approved public wording.

## Categories
AI, AWS, cloud, DevOps, containers, languages, operating systems, data, identity, security, observability, architecture, consulting, documentation, leadership, and industry knowledge.

## Rules
Ratings must follow evidence, not aspiration. Separate recent capability from historical experience. Do not use the matrix publicly without adapting it to the audience.
