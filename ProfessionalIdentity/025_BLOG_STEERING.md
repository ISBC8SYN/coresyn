# 025_BLOG_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern long-form publishing that builds discoverability, trust, technical proof, and consulting demand.

## Content Pillars
AI platform architecture, AWS design, DevOps reliability, automation, identity, observability, architecture tradeoffs, documentation, and resilient operations.

## Article Formula
Specific audience problem, concise answer, context, implementation pattern, example, risks, checklist, and CTA.

## Editorial Rules
Publish useful material rather than promotional filler. Cite external facts, test technical examples, disclose assumptions, and avoid confidential information.

## Cadence
Prefer one strong article every two weeks plus derivative short posts. Maintain an editorial backlog ranked by audience value, evidence availability, and commercial relevance.

## CTA
Invite readers to review a case study, book an assessment, or discuss a related architecture problem.
