# 033_ENTERPRISE_ROADMAP_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern phased enterprise modernization and platform roadmaps.

## Roadmap Layers
Current state, target outcomes, constraints, capability gaps, workstreams, dependencies, milestones, risks, governance, adoption, measurement, and operating ownership.

## Horizon Model
- 0–30 days: assessment and critical stabilization
- 31–90 days: foundational controls and pilot delivery
- 3–6 months: scaled implementation and operating model
- 6–12 months: optimization, productization, and expansion

## Prioritization
Rank initiatives by safety, business impact, urgency, dependency, evidence, effort, reversibility, and total cost of ownership.

## Required Outputs
Executive roadmap, technical roadmap, dependency map, risk register, decision log, cost assumptions, success metrics, and review cadence.

## Rule
A roadmap is a decision instrument, not a promise. Label assumptions, owners, funding dependencies, and unresolved decisions.
