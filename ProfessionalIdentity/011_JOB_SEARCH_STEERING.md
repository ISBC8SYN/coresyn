# 011_JOB_SEARCH_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Create a measurable job-search system for remote AI platform, AWS, cloud, infrastructure, DevOps, and architecture roles.

## Target Roles
Principal AI Platform Architect, AWS Solutions Architect, Cloud Architect, Platform Architect, Infrastructure Architect, DevOps Architect, Systems Integration Architect, and senior consulting equivalents.

## Application Rules
Prioritize fit over volume. Tailor title, summary, top skills, and first five bullets to each role. Never invent credentials or experience.

## Daily Operating Cycle
1. Review pipeline
2. Identify 10–20 high-fit roles
3. Tailor résumé
4. Submit
5. Record status
6. Contact recruiter or hiring manager where appropriate
7. Follow up after 3–5 business days
8. Review conversion data weekly

## Tracking Fields
Company, role, source, date, compensation, location, contact, résumé version, keywords, status, follow-up date, interview notes, rejection reason, and next action.

## Interview Preparation
Prepare evidence stories for architecture, AWS, AI integration, CI/CD, automation, incident handling, tradeoffs, stakeholder communication, security, and failure recovery.

## Disability Disclosure
Disclosure is Joshua’s choice. Accommodation requests should be specific, functional, and separate from technical qualifications.

## Success Metrics
Track qualified applications, recruiter replies, screening calls, technical interviews, final interviews, offers, and time-to-response. Optimize for conversion, not raw application count.

## Master Prompt
Use the governing steering files and verified evidence to tailor materials for the role. Preserve truth, improve keyword alignment, and make the strongest relevant evidence visible in the first half of the résumé.
