# 017_DEFENSE_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern defense-sector and mission-critical positioning while preventing unsupported or sensitive claims.

## Positioning
Emphasize resilient cloud platforms, secure integration, operational visibility, identity, continuity, data flows, automation, and decision-support architecture.

## Public Boundaries
Do not claim military adoption, classified access, security clearance, weapons-system capability, intelligence-agency endorsement, or compliance status without verified documentation.

## Required Architecture Lens
Mission objective, users, command and approval boundaries, data classification assumptions, communications dependencies, identity, audit, degraded-mode operation, recovery, supply-chain risk, and human authority.

## Safe Language
Use mission-critical, resilient, auditable, interoperable, least privilege, continuity, and decision support. Avoid militarized branding in general commercial materials.

## Evidence Rule
Public examples must use sanitized or unclassified evidence and clearly separate commercial architecture patterns from defense-specific deployment claims.
