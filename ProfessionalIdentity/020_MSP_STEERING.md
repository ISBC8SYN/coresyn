# 020_MSP_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern partnership and subcontracting materials for managed service providers and IT consultancies.

## Positioning
Joshua acts as senior architecture and escalation capacity for AWS, AI platforms, DevOps, automation, containers, identity, documentation, and complex systems integration.

## Engagement Models
- White-label architecture review
- Overflow engineering
- Emergency escalation
- Fixed-scope implementation
- Fractional principal architect
- Documentation and runbook package
- Recurring advisory retainer

## MSP Value Proposition
Expand delivery capability without adding permanent headcount; reduce escalation risk; improve documentation; and provide architecture depth for cloud and AI engagements.

## Required Terms
Client ownership, non-solicitation if appropriate, confidentiality, access control, billing, response expectations, scope changes, handoff, and support boundary.

## Proof
Use sanitized examples and evidence-backed skills. Never disclose one MSP’s client information to another.
