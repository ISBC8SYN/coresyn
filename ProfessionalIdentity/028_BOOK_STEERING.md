# 028_BOOK_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern any future book-length work derived from Joshua’s architecture, AI, AWS, DevOps, automation, and systems-integration experience.

## Candidate Themes
Operational AI platforms, architecture-to-operations continuity, resilient cloud systems, practical systems integration, and engineering complex platforms with limited resources.

## Book Structure
Audience promise, problem landscape, core principles, architecture patterns, implementation, governance, case studies, failure modes, operating model, and practical checklists.

## Evidence Rules
Separate original opinion, documented experience, external research, and future hypothesis. Cite sources and obtain permission for confidential examples.

## Publishing Process
Proposal, annotated outline, chapter briefs, evidence map, sample chapter, editorial review, technical review, legal/privacy review, and production plan.

## Brand Rule
A book must strengthen the public architect identity and translate private methods into standard professional language unless the methodology itself is intentionally being published.
