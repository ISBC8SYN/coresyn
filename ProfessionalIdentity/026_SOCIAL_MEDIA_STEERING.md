# 026_SOCIAL_MEDIA_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern professional social content across LinkedIn and other public channels.

## Objectives
Build credibility, demonstrate useful thinking, create conversations, and direct qualified readers to portfolio, services, or job opportunities.

## Content Mix
- Practical architecture lessons
- Short technical explainers
- Case-study excerpts
- Failure and recovery lessons
- Tool comparisons with context
- Professional milestones
- Questions that invite informed discussion

## Rules
Use a calm professional tone. Do not publish personal disputes, unverified accusations, confidential data, private methodology names, or reactive arguments from the professional account.

## Post Formula
Hook, useful point, concrete example, implication, and one relevant CTA or question.

## Frequency
Favor consistency over volume. Suggested baseline: two substantive posts and several thoughtful comments per week.
