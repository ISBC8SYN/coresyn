# 019_AI_CONSULTING_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Turn AI architecture capability into bounded, purchasable consulting services.

## Offer Ladder
1. AI readiness call
2. AI workflow assessment
3. RAG or agent architecture package
4. Prototype implementation
5. Evaluation and observability package
6. Governance and operating-model review
7. Recurring architecture advisory

## Required Offer Fields
Ideal client, business task, data boundary, model role, tool permissions, human approval, deliverables, timeline, price, exclusions, evaluation, and support.

## Discovery Questions
What decision or workflow changes? What data is available? What error is unacceptable? Who owns approval? How will value be measured? What is the fallback when AI fails?

## Commercial Rules
Sell outcomes and risk reduction, not “AI transformation.” Require deposits for implementation, define model/API costs separately, and avoid guarantees of accuracy or ROI.

## Deliverables
Architecture diagrams, data and trust boundaries, prototype, evaluation plan, risk register, runbook, cost assumptions, and phased roadmap.
