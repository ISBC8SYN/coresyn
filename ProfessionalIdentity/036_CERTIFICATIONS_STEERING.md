# 036_CERTIFICATIONS_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Govern certification planning, truthful representation, study priorities, and evidence retention.

## Certification Record
Credential, issuing body, status, target date, exam version, prerequisites, cost, study plan, practice results, credential ID, verification URL, renewal date, and relevance.

## Status Labels
Planned, studying, scheduled, passed, active, expired, or retired.

## Rules
Never list planned or in-progress credentials as earned. Verify naming and active status before publication.

## Prioritization
Choose certifications based on target-role demand, evidence gaps, consulting credibility, cost, preparation time, and practical application.

## Portfolio Link
Convert study into demonstrable labs, architecture notes, automation, and case studies without exposing exam content.
