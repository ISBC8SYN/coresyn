# 038_ACCOMPLISHMENT_MATRIX_STEERING.md

## Purpose
Maintain a defensible inventory of accomplishments for résumés, interviews, portfolios, proposals, and biographies.

## Accomplishment Record
- Accomplishment ID
- Role/project
- Date
- Problem
- Scope
- Action
- Result
- Metric and source
- Technologies
- Stakeholders
- Evidence paths
- Disclosure status
- Approved résumé bullet
- Approved interview story
- Missing proof

## Rules
Use measured outcomes where available. When metrics are absent, state the delivered capability or risk reduction without fabricating numbers.

## Selection
Choose accomplishments by relevance, recency, difficulty, ownership, business value, evidence strength, and uniqueness.
